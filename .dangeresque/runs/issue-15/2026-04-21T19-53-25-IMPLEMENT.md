- Passed `startedAtMs: runStartedAtMs` into the `ArtifactBuilder` constructor call (line 286).

`workerStartedAtMs` on line 264 is untouched — it is still captured immediately before `await runWorker(...)` and remains the correct input to `setWorkerTiming`.
