Added one here because the WHY is genuinely non-obvious: `-D` looks over-eager if you don't know that Phase 1 already enforces the real invariant. Without this comment, a future reader could easily revert `-D` → `-d` on "defensive" grounds and silently reintroduce the bug. Meets the "hidden invariant" bar.
