## Summary

Issue #17 asked whether `claude-opus-4-7[1m]` (and similar bracketed model names) would break dangeresque. Answer: **no**. The `[` / `]` characters never reach a shell — they ride through argv arrays to `spawn("claude", args, ...)` / `spawn("codex", args, ...)` verbatim. All shell-interpreted calls (`execSync` / `spawnSync({ shell: true })`) in the codebase are git/gh operations that do NOT include the model string.
